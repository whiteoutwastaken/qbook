# Islamic Works and Contributions

## Overview
- **Islamic works** encompass a broad range of literature and art developed over centuries.
- These works include:
  - Religious texts
  - Poetry
  - Philosophy
  - Scientific treatises

## Key Texts
- **Quran**: The central religious text of Islam, written in Arabic and considered a masterpiece of the language.
- **Hadith**: Complement the Quran by providing context and practical examples of living according to Islamic principles.
  - Cover topics from personal hygiene to social justice.
  - Essential for understanding Islamic law and ethics.

## Islamic Philosophy
- Significant development in Islamic philosophy by scholars like:
  - **Al-Ghazali**
  - **Ibn Rushd**
- Engaged with Greek philosophy, integrating ideas from:
  - Aristotle
  - Plato
- This exchange shaped both Islamic and Western philosophical traditions.

## Islamic Art and Architecture
- **Islamic art**:
  - Includes calligraphy, geometric patterns, and floral designs.
  - Avoids depicting living beings, focusing on abstract and symbolic representations.
- **Islamic architecture**:
  - Distinctive features include domes, arches, and intricate tile work.
  - Notable examples:
    - Mosques
    - Palaces
    - Madrasas

## Islamic Science
- Made significant contributions to fields such as:
  - Mathematics
  - Astronomy
  - Medicine
- Prominent scholars:
  - **Al-Khwarizmi**: Known as the father of algebra.
  - **Al-Razi**: A prominent physician.
- Their works were translated into Latin and other languages, influencing the Renaissance in Europe.
- Important discoveries in:
  - Optics
  - Chemistry
  - Geography
- Left a lasting legacy in the history of science.
